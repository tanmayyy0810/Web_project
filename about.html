About Me

Hello! I'm Tanmay Rastogi, a Computer Science undergraduate at Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) [Reg No: 24BCE2994]. I am a dedicated developer with a strong passion for creating innovative solutions through code, particularly in the areas of software development, machine learning, and IoT systems.

My technical journey involves working with languages like Java, C++, and Python, alongside foundational web technologies. I enjoy tackling complex problems and turning ideas into functional software. Beyond the screen, I actively participate in student organizations to strengthen my problem-solving and collaboration skills. Currently, I serve as a Senior Core Member and PR Assistant for ISTE at VIT Vellore, where I enjoy sharing knowledge and connecting with the developer community.

I'm always eager to learn new technologies and collaborate on exciting projects. Feel free to explore my portfolio and get in touch if you'd like to work together!
